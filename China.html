The Industrial Powerhouse (~40% of GDP)

Manufacturing remains the bedrock, but it's a story of evolution. A strategic focus on high-tech areas like EVs, AI, and renewables is transforming our industrial base, while construction continues to support massive infrastructure and urbanization projects.

The Services Backbone (~54% of GDP)

The services sector is now the largest part of our economy, reflecting a shift to a consumption-led model. The boom in digital platforms (Wholesale & Retail), a sophisticated financial system, and a growing IT industry are central to this transformation.
